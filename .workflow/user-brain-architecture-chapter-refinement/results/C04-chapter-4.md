## Integration Notes
Frame the ledger as a learning receipt book: what the learner did, what was
observed, and why that is enough to update the learner model.

## Recommended Shape
- The learner brain is not a mood, guess, or hidden GPT impression.
- Explain the three questions every change should answer.
- Translate episodic memory as session memory and semantic memory as lasting
  understanding.
- Make soft signals lower-weight: hesitation can adjust teaching style but not
  prove mastery.

## Caveats
GPT may propose observations, but durable mastery is evidence-driven. Do not
overstate pauses or confidence.

## Plain-English Sentence
The learner brain should only change its long-term picture of a learner when it
can point to what happened and why that evidence matters.
