# Packet P5: Evaluator

## Objective
Evaluate P1/P2 findings and the P3/P4 debate. Decide what belongs in the final
book and what should be rejected or caveated.

## Do
- Use a clear rubric: truth, implementation feasibility, reader clarity,
  pedagogical value, cost/latency risk, and source strength.
- Prefer concrete book changes over broad advice.
- Identify any remaining unresolved risks.

## Do Not
- Merely summarize the debate.
- Accept unsourced claims.

## Expected Output
Final evaluation with accepted/rejected/conflict decisions.
